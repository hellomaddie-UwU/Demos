The Importance of Profitability in Agreements

Agreement profitability reveals how much profit each service contract truly delivers, after factoring in labor and tools. It highlights which agreements are worth investing in, and which are silently draining your resources.

Low-margin contracts often signal deeper problems, such as operational inefficiencies, underpricing, or recurring issues that degrade service quality and increase churn. By surfacing these insights, you can double down on what works, restructure services, and price smarter to grow sustainably.
