## 1. Welcome Screen Design Guidelines
**Client:** MSPbots

A self-initiated design system extension for MSPbots, built to close a gap that should have been addressed during the original build. These guidelines give the Asset Team a single source of truth for designing consistent Welcome Screens across every app in the MSPbots suite, replacing ad hoc, one-off decisions with a repeatable standard.

## 2. Multi-Product Launch Pages
**Client:** MSPbots

MSPbots regularly upgrades or launches new apps based on churn-rate data, and each release needed a landing page to match. These pages communicate each app's benefits, features, and value at a glance, with the explicit goal of converting prospects into booked demos with a Customer Success Manager.

---

## 3. A UX Audit and Site Revamp
**Client:** Korn Ferry

Years ago, Korn Ferry's outdated site was successfully rebuilt to better showcase their course catalog. Since then, the client migrated to Webflow and the experience quietly regressed, leaving multiple broken 404 pages and poor navigation. This demo audits the current Webflow site and proposes an updated design that fixes the UX issues while staying true to Korn Ferry's existing branding and intent.

---

## 4. Business Launched Website
**Client:** Joblu

Joblu was an early-stage startup that needed a marketing site to educate prospects on its key selling points, a project that succeeded at launch. A later revamp by another team weakened both the visual design and conversion performance, so this demo presents a proposed revamp grounded in deep familiarity with the brand, having also led Joblu's brand system, mobile and web app design, and marketing at the time.

---

## 5. MVP Build
**Client:** Kuushy

Kuushy was a successfully funded Kickstarter project for an AI-powered sensory seat cushion designed to ease back pain for office workers. The scope covered both the brand mascot and the full MVP app design. Though the product is no longer on the market, this interactive demo preserves the app experience as originally designed.

---

## 6. Dashboard Revamp
**Client:** Vyepti *(card currently lists "Lundbeck" — see note below)*

Vyepti is a booking web app that lets nurses and administrative staff schedule treatments with the appropriate doctor. The original build succeeded but drifted from the client's own brief as direction shifted mid-project, leaving gaps between what was promised and what shipped. This revamp restores every originally specified function in a cleaner, more clinical interface suited to a medical product.
